    What led us to create this site?

    Currently , there are many problems related to the composition of food! We tried to take universal human problems as our main focus! Since food is the daily source of human energy, the purity of the food composition is one of the main factors!

    The biggest problem we face in our daily life is that the food we eat is not halal, the presence of dubious additives in its composition! To prevent such moments, we recommend this website!
