    What you mean by halal product?

    In Islam, Halal is an Arabic term meaning “lawful or permissible” and not only encompasses food and drink, but all matters of daily life. The opposite of Halal is Haram, which means “unlawful, not permissible or prohibited.

    The quality of Halal food and other products attracts a variety of consumers all around the globe. Anyone regardless their faith, gender, ethnicity, nationality, race, age- can eat ‘Halal’ and lead a ‘Halal’ lifestyle.

    With regards to the term “Halal” within the Food & Beverage Industry, it simply refers to anything permissible to consume. If it is used in reference to animal meat, then it means any animal which has been slaughtered in accordance with the Shariah method is Halal (lawful) to consume.

    List of Haram or not permitted

    • Alcohol – intoxicants and narcotic drugs

    • Swine and all pork by-products and/or their derivatives

    • Animal Fat - may be consumed if derived from Halal slaughtered animals

    • Blood and blood by-products - blood is the circulatory cleansing fluid of the body and is not to be consumed

    • Carnivorous animals, birds of prey, scavengers, and animals improperly slaughtered, sick or dying before slaughter

    • Idolatry - any animals or poultry sacrificed for voodoo, witchcraft or practice that denies the existence of the Creator are not to be consumed.

    • Foods contaminated with any of the above products or with "impurities” from processing, such as manure, urine, rodent droppings, infectious fluids, or pus are considered Haram.

    Benefits of eating Halal products

    1. Safer to Eat
    In Halal food, great emphasis is placed on food safety and hygiene. This means that there is also a lower incidence rate when it comes to food contamination

    2. More Ethical
    It is not permissible to slaughter an animal that is sick, so animals are kept in natural and clean conditions to support disease-free growth. Animals are treated well, causing animals less stress and pain compared to modern farming techniques

    3. Improves Metabolism
    Healthy consumption supports the immune system, brain and metabolic function. And metabolic function is vital in maintaining the body’s overall health
